Privacy Policy

Chrome Extension · Last updated: June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Space DJ browser extension ("the Extension", "we", "us") handles information when you use it. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information We Collect

Space DJ does not collect, store, sell, or transmit any personally identifiable information. The Extension does not track your browsing history, and it does not require you to create an account.

Audio and Permissions

Space DJ processes audio locally within your browser to provide its functionality. Any audio captured or shared by the Extension is handled on your device and is used solely to deliver the feature you requested. We do not record, retain, or upload your audio to our servers.

How We Use Permissions

The Extension only requests the browser permissions necessary for its core features. These permissions are used exclusively to operate the Extension and are never used to collect data about you.

Third-Party Services

Space DJ does not share data with third-party advertisers or analytics providers. If the Extension interacts with a third-party service to provide its functionality, that interaction is limited to what is required to perform the action you initiated.

Data Security

Because Space DJ does not collect or store your personal data, there is no personal information for us to expose. Any data processed by the Extension stays on your device.

Children's Privacy

Space DJ is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page along with an updated revision date.

Contact Us
